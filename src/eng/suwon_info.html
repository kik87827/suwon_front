  • Geographical Position of Suwon

    Suwon is located in the central part of the Korean Peninsula (longitude 127° E, latitude 37° N), and its terrain gently slopes from northeast to southwest. In the center of the city, there is Mount Paldalsan, which stands at an elevation of 143 meters, while to the north, there is the majestic Gwanggyosan, and to the west, Chilbosan forms a natural backdrop. The southern area comprises flat plains.

    Suwon experiences a temperate climate, with an annual average temperature of 13ºC and an annual precipitation of around 370mm. The first frost typically occurs around October 20th.
