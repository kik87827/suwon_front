Suwon Convention Center Address

By Subway

「Gwanggyo Jungang(Ajou Univ.) Station」, Sinbundang Line

  • Get off Gwanggyo Jungang Station(Exit 4)
  • Walk to Suwon Convention Center(Approx. 10 minutes on foot)

By Bus

Express Bus Stop: 「Gwanggyo Jungang·Ajou University Transfer Center」

  • Seoul Station M5115, M5121 / Gangnam Station M5422
  • Get off at Gwanggyo Jungang·Ajou University Transfer Center(Exit 4) Walk to Suwon Convention Center(Approx. 10 minutes on foot)

Intra-city Bus Stop : 「Suwon Convention Center · Lotte Outlet」

  • 5-4, 19, 20, 32, 32-3, 32-4, 670, 720-3, 999
  • Get off at Suwon Convention Center · Lotte Outlet Walk to Suwon Convention Center (Approx. 3 minutes on foot)

By Airport Limousine Bus

  • Take Bus No. 8877(60~80 minutes)
  • Get off at Gwanggyo Jungang· Ajou Daeyeok Transfer Center(Exit 4)
  • Suwon Convention Center(Approx. 10 minutes on foot)

Gimpo International Airport : Intercity Bus for 「Dongsuwon Station」
