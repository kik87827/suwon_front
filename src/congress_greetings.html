Greetings

  • Mayor of Suwon
  • AP BOD

Mayor of Suwon

I am delighted that the 20th Asia-Pacific ITS (Intelligent Transportation Systems) Conference will be held in Suwon.

Suwon, a city with a population of 1.25 million, is not only a historical city embracing UNESCO World Heritage but also a hub for advanced industries with companies like Samsung Electronics and CJ. Over the years, Suwon has successfully hosted global mega-events, starting with the 2002 FIFA World Cup, followed by the U-20 World Cup, and the Asia-Pacific Environment Ministers' Meeting, showcasing its wealth of experience.

Furthermore, Suwon holds the distinction of being South Korea's first ITS city, with a 26-year history of understanding and implementing Intelligent Transportation Systems, which makes it an ideal host for this conference.

Under the theme "Hyper-Connected," Suwon aims to prepare for a future mobility society where cities, people, and technology are interconnected.

The 20th Asia-Pacific ITS Conference in Suwon will offer a diverse range of programs, including academic sessions, exhibitions, Suwon ITS Town, business matching, and pre-conference events, ensuring an enjoyable experience for all participants.

Moreover, Suwon boasts an intelligent transportation system and preserves beautiful historical and cultural sites, allowing visitors to experience South Korea's unique culture.

I hope that the 20th Asia-Pacific ITS Conference in Suwon will be a turning point for the ITS field, providing a great opportunity for sharing knowledge and insights from various countries. I also hope it serves as a platform for policymakers from different nations to engage in discussions and dialogue about the future of ITS.

Suwon City is committed to creating the best environment for a successful conference and your active participation. We look forward to welcoming you to the 20th Suwon Asia-Pacific ITS Conference.

AP BOD

The Korean government initiated the introduction of Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS) in the 1990s to address transportation challenges arising from economic growth, rapid population expansion, and an increase in vehicles.

Starting with the enactment of the Transportation System Efficiency Act in 1999, we have witnessed remarkable progress and achievements, continuously advancing towards future ITS technologies such as C-ITS and autonomous driving.

While we began as a latecomer to ITS compared to other nations, we have now emerged as a leading country in ITS technology export.

Suwon, as the first city in South Korea to introduce ITS, is proud to be recognized as an exemplary ITS city, emphasizing citizens' convenience and safety, including the pioneering introduction of the emergency vehicle priority signal system.

It is an honor to announce that the 20th Asia-Pacific ITS Conference will be held in Suwon, South Korea, the very city that pioneered ITS in our country. Anticipated to draw around 2,500 participants from approximately 20 countries, the conference, scheduled from May 12 to 14, 2025, promises to be an event of unprecedented scale and substance.

Not only in terms of size but also in content, this conference will be rich and diverse, featuring academic programs, exhibitions, business consultations, and the Suwon ITS Town. It will provide a platform for each attendee to showcase their innovative technologies and share knowledge, making it an "opportunity space."

Particularly, the theme of the 20th Asia-Pacific ITS Conference, "Hyper-Connected," will allow attendees to witness the latest ITS technologies and mobility services in Korea during technology showcases.

Suwon is making every effort to ensure that the 20th Asia-Pacific ITS Conference in Suwon will be of the highest quality and yield significant results.

As the representative of ITS in the Asia-Pacific region, we extend a warm invitation to all of you to join us at the 20th Asia-Pacific ITS Conference in Suwon.

Chairman of ITS Korea

The Asia-Pacific ITS (Intelligent Transportation Systems) Conference serves as an international event for the exchange of ITS technology within the Asia-Pacific region.

Since its inception with the 1st Tokyo ITS Conference in 1996, the ITS AP Secretariat has been organizing this event every three years, bringing together experts from various countries. The conference provides a platform for sharing knowledge on ITS policies, technology, and the latest updates.

Hosting the ITS Asia-Pacific Conference in South Korea is a significant event, marking the first time since the 5th Seoul ITS Conference in 2002, a gap of 23 years. Suwon, the host city for the 2025 ITS Asia-Pacific Conference, was the first city in South Korea to adopt ITS. Leveraging its experience in infrastructure development over the years, Suwon aims to present solutions to urban transportation challenges through ITS.

The 20th Suwon ITS Asia-Pacific Conference, themed "Hyper-Connected," intends to showcase a variety of cutting-edge technologies and future services while highlighting South Korea's outstanding ITS technology.

Moreover, we aspire to create a platform for sharing diverse knowledge on ITS policies and technologies accumulated by countries in the Asia-Pacific region.

To ensure the successful organization of the 20th Suwon ITS Asia-Pacific Conference, ITS Korea is collaborating not only with the Ministry of Land, Infrastructure, and Transport, Suwon City, private enterprises, and academia but also with international institutions such as the ITS AP Secretariat and member countries.

We look forward to the participation of many individuals at the 20th Suwon ITS Asia-Pacific Conference.

Please mark your calendars for May 12-14, 2025, and do not forget to join this regional celebration.

We hope to see you all at the conference. Thank you.
